Do you know your Purpose Piece?

We all have a distinct way of showing up when things need to get done — a specific “shape” we take that makes a project, a team, or a community click into place.

Most of us do it instinctively, but we rarely have a name for it.

It isn’t a personality test. It’s pattern recognition.

This isn’t about your traits or your preferences. By looking at how you naturally solve problems and inhabit space, we identify the specific role you play in the larger whole.

Less about who you are. More about the unique value you add.

How it works

We’re going to walk through a series of prompts designed to surface your natural patterns. There are no right answers, only honest ones.

By the end, you’ll have a clearer picture of where you fit in the work ahead.
